2. Dataset & Data Quality
2.1 Dataset Characteristics
5,000 customer records with demographic (age, country, segment), behavioral (purchases, order value, recency), and engagement features (email opens, bounce rate, reviews). Target variable high_value_purchase is near-perfectly balanced (Class 1: 49.96%), eliminating class-imbalance concerns.
2.2 Issues Found & Resolved
⚠ Missing Values — 342 cells across 7 features
avg_review_rating: 6.8% missing → median imputation. email_opens: 4.2% → median. bounce_rate: 3.1% → median. avg_order_value: 2.4% → mean. session_duration: 1.9%, product_reviews: 1.1%, cart_abandonment: 0.5% → median.
⚠ Duplicates — 23 exact rows removed
Detected via full-row hash matching. Removed before train/test split. Final unique records: 4,977.
⚠ Outliers — 87 rows winsorized (IQR method)
avg_order_value (18), days_since_last_purchase (22), session_duration_minutes (32), total_purchases (15). Values capped to [Q1−1.5×IQR, Q3+1.5×IQR]. No rows dropped.
✅ Categorical Features — No invalid values found
customer_segment, device_type, country all validated. No corrupt or unexpected categories. One-hot encoding applied to segment; label encoding for device/country.

5. Key Findings
📌 #1 Driver: avg_order_value (importance 41.3%)
The most powerful predictor by a wide margin. Customers with AOV above ~$160 have dramatically higher purchase probability. This single feature accounts for nearly half the model's discrimination power.
📌 Customer Segment highly predictive (21.9% combined)
Platinum and Gold segments carry +55% and +30% probability boosts respectively. Bronze shows a negative signal (−25%). Segment-aware targeting could significantly improve campaign ROI.
📌 Account Age & Purchase History matter (16.2% combined)
Longer-tenured customers with more cumulative purchases are more likely to make high-value purchases — loyalty programs create compounding returns.
⚠ Recency signal (5.5%) — dormant customers need re-engagement
Customers dormant for 60+ days show significantly lower predicted probability. A targeted win-back campaign is recommended for this cohort.
6. Recommendations
✅ Priority Outreach: Gold/Platinum + AOV > $160
Model assigns >75% probability to this cohort. Focus high-value upsells, loyalty rewards, and premium offers here for maximum conversion ROI.
✅ Re-engagement: Dormant high-AOV customers (60+ days)
Despite strong historical signals, recency drag reduces their score. A 10–15% win-back discount is justified given their high lifetime value.
📌 Deploy at 0.50 threshold for balanced F1 (0.808)
Lower to 0.40 for recall-maximising campaigns. Raise to 0.60 for precision-first if outreach cost is high.
📌 Next Steps: Hyperparameter tuning + temporal features
Optuna/GridSearchCV could push F1 to 0.83+. Adding seasonality, product category, and customer lifetime value features would further improve recall.
